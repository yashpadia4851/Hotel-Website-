Welcone to Yash Hotel

Hotel Yash is located at a distance of 3 km from Ashapura Temple and 45 km from Deodar Bus Stand. This hotel in Patan has an in-house restaurant within the premises. It comprises of 23 spacious and well-kept rooms spread across 3 floors. Rooms are well-equipped with amenities like bottled drinking water, welcome amenities, room heater,wake-up calls, temperature control and wardrobe. It has an attached bathroom with hot and cold running water facility. This hotel in Patan offers 24-hour front desk facility along with services like laundry, medical assistance, welcome amenities, round the clock security and room service for limited hours
